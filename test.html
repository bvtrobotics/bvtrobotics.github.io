BVT Robotics Newsletter | 7/21/19
Welcome Back!
So far this year, we have been building up fundamental skills of building a robot in order to create team members who will be able to understand and work with Robots. We have taught new members the basics of driving and building robots using simple clawbots to help start them out. This week we have just started teaching about engineering principles and engineering notebooks through the reverse engineering of the first robot. We are now working with Douglas middle school to help in mentoring their Vex IQ teams. The snap raise fundraiser is now over and cannot take any more submissions. The programing team meets in IT on Tuesdays and Thursdays. They are currently discussing and learning about how to code in JavaScript. They have recently updated the code in the camera system to allow our robot to find and drive to reflective strips.

Mission Statement
To order to positively influence and inspire our community, peers, and younger students, as well as teach them about the impact their work could have on the future, BVT Robotics plans to spread our knowledge about science, engineering, and technology to through exciting mentor-based programs and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, interpersonal communication skills, and leadership development.
